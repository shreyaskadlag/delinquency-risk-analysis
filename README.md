# 📊 Delinquency Risk & Collections Analytics

## 🔍 Project Overview

This project analyzes credit and customer data to identify patterns of delinquency and assess risk levels for better collections strategy.

The objective is to detect high-risk customers early and support data-driven financial decision-making.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Python (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
* Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
* Basic Risk Segmentation

---

## 📊 Data Description

* Customer financial data
* Payment history and delinquency indicators
* Income and behavioral attributes

---

## 📌 Key Analysis Performed

* Data cleaning and preprocessing
* Identification of missing values and inconsistencies
* Analysis of:

  * Payment delays
  * Income distribution
  * Customer behavior patterns

---

## 📈 Risk Segmentation Approach

Customers were categorized based on simple business rules:

* High Risk → Low income + frequent late payments
* Medium Risk → Moderate income + occasional delays
* Low Risk → Stable income + consistent payments
